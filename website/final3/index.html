Hey there!

We proudly present to you "voce per tutti", your one and only stop for synthesized speech for the underrepresented languages and dialect

Our intentions:

  • We aim to provide a sustainable solution for preserving language diversity.
  • Statistics suggest that there are huge caveats when it comes to the domination of certain languages over the others, even in major domains such as education, we are here to change that.
  • Lastly, this platform is completely open source with the plain and simple intention of levelling the playing field for all :)

Our Features:

01

Assortment of Languages to choose from

We assure smooth conversion from text to speech for over 50 languages and 10 dialects

02

Translation

We have also provided the functionality where one can translate a given text from one language to the other

03

Surplus to Requirements?!

yeah we do speech recognition too !
